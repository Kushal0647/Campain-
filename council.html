Student Council

Student Council at Campion College are non-political, officially elected or appointed body of Campion students which function under the guidance of the student council Moderator for the welfare and discipline of the Academy.

1) Main functions:

  • To represent students to the Campion Management
  • To assist management in organizing different extra-curricular activities viz. Student Get Together, Welcome Parties, Talent Nite, Spring Festival, Elocution, Debates, Talk Programs, Sports etc.
  • To publish the Campionite, Campion Newsletter and Magazine etc.

2) Aims and Onjectives:

  • To encourage students to continually strive for excellence in every field.
  • To value and judiciously use their freedom.
  • To live, lead and serve their fellow countrymen and country selflessy.
  • To grow into dynamic citizens of the country.
  • To exploit the hidden talents and use them to build the nation.
  • To cultivate a sense of duty and responsibility to shoulder the task of the nation building.
  • To accentuate and strive the achieved all-round personality development and be able to participate in the national and international affairs.

3) Structure:
  • The structure of the council shall be a three-tired one.
  • THE EXECUTIVE: The executive shall be the Legislative Wing of the Council. The ececutive consists of 4 permanent members whose term of office shall be of one year. This includes the; PRESIDENT(1), VICE PRESIDENT(1), GENERAL SECRETARY(1), and TREASURER(1).
  • GENERAL BODY: All the elected and nominated members shall make up the General body of the student.
