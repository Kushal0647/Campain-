Campion College Admission

Admission

1) Admission Form

Application Forms and Prospectus can be obtained from the college Administration Office. Application forms should reach the college office by the submission deadline, failing which the College will not be responsible for admission.

2) Admission Requistes

    The applicant should enclose the following with the applicant form :
  • Certificates/testimonials of 10+2 equivalent examinations passed.
  • Equivalence, Transfer, Character and Original Migration Certificates.
  • Passport Photographs.

3) Admission Test

Applicants will be required to appear for an admission test designed to evaluate their abilities and aptitude for the program as per T.U. Entrance Test.

    The admission test for students applying for the B.B.S. program will be held on the following areas
  1. Verbal-ability
  2. Quantitative ability
  3. Business and Economics
  4. Logical reasoning
  5. General awareness

    The admission test for students applying for the B.Sc. program will be held on the following areas :
  1. Physics
  2. Mathematics
  3. Chemistry

    Similarly, the admission test for students applying for the B.A. program will be held on following areas :
  1. English
  2. Nepali
  3. Major Subject areas (any two).
Date and time for admission tests will be announced and or placed on the College Notice Board

4) Interview

Those applicants who have obtained the minimum marks required for admission in the admission test will be required to appear for an interview before members of the Admission Committee. Candidate selected for the admission will have to produce original certificates for all examinations at the time of interview.

5) Selection

Final selection for admission will be based on the applicantâ€™s previous academic performance, marks obtained in the admission test and the interview evaluations. Applicants who fulfill the admission criteria are eligible for registration in Campion and their names will be placed at the College notice board. The right of admission shall be that of the Campion College Admissions Committee.

6)Registration and Documents

The successful applicants must pay the installment fee within 10 days of the publication of the selection list.All the required documents must be submitted at the time of registration. In case of delay in fees deposit and/or in submitting the required documents within 10 days the college reserves the right to cancel admission.
