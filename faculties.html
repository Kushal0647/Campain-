Faculty Of Management (Bachelor in Business Studies)

The faculty of Management is one of the three faculties at Campion College. It offers a 3â€“year Bachelor in Business Studies (B.B.S) degree in affilation with Tribhuvan University which is the Nepal's number one University.

The curriculum for 3-year BBS degree comprises of four separate and distinct course components:

  • A strong foundation in allied areas of business such as language, economic analyses, legal environment and quantitative to understand, analyze and comprehend the management concepts, theories and practices
  • Core business studies encompassing and integrating all functional areas to provide graduates with an appreciation of the diversity and inter-relationship of business and management issues
  • The opportunity to choose any sectoral management area such as tourism, cooperatives, transport, hot small business, and banking as an elective course to enable graduate to apply the core and functional knowledge and skills in their chosen sector of business.
  • The oppertunity to choose one specilized area on their third year program such as Accounting, Finance, Marketting in order to provide graduates with some degree of function expertise in their own interest.

Note :

To Admit in three year Bachlor in Business Studies, the candidates must had successfully completed intermediate level of 10+2 with Mathmetics or Economics and English as two full papers.
