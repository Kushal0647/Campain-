Fee Regulation

  1. The tuition fees are different for each of the faculties at the College, and the fee amounts are given in fee strusture attached with the prospectus.
  2. The 1st installment should be paid at the time of Admission
  3. The 2nd installment shound be paid before 15th November
  4. The 3rd installment shound be paid before 15th Feburary . All the fees should be paid before the sent-up examination
  5. Late Fee at the rate of 2% per month on the total amount shall be charged on delinquent account. Student failing to clear fees within the due date of payment will be warned and may be expleed from the College
  6. The Comprehensive fee does not cover costs of Uniform, Books, Handouts, Photocopies and personal expenses
  7. Parents or Guardians should come to deposite College fee because College will not be responsible for the loss of money brought by student
  8. A student who dicontinues his/her studies or is expelled during the course will not get any of the fees refunded
  9. Campion Management reserves the right to change or amend the fees as when necessary at the begenning of each academic year
