Other Facilities

1) Computer Lab
Campion College is faciliated with one of the most advanced computer. It has got fifty computers netwoked with eachother. Student are encourage to make maximum use of the Computer Lab. Here are the some picture of computer lab.

2) Library
Library of Campion College fully caters to the needs of the teachers and Students with course books and references. Over 5,000 books by native and foreign writers are available in the Library. All the students are encourage to make maximum utilization of the library facilities. Book or documents borrowed from the Library have to be returned on time. In case of loss of books or any damage done to the library property the comcerned student shall be responsible for the loss or damage.

3) Transportation
Campion College has got three good condition, standard buses for the transportation. Pick-up and drop by bus within the Ring Road of Kathmandu Valley is available at reasonable rate. The routs are aligned for convenience of all the students and to save time for pick-up and drop, bus service is on major roads only. The College makes sure that the transport service runs without fail to pick-up students at defined points.

4) Cafeteria
Campion College has got Roof Top Cafeteria for lunch and light refreshment at reasonable prices. Cafeteria foods are vey hygienic, we can also order the food of our own choice but we have to ask for it in advance. Facilities provided are exclusive for Campion Student and Staffs.
