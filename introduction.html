INTRODUCTION

Campion Foundation is named after Edmund Campion, one of the prominent Jesuit Educators. The college was established in 1997 as an integral part of various other Campion undertakings to impart quality education to those students who have passed 10+2 or equivalent examinations. Campion College currently offers three year degrees in three different streams: Bachelor in Business Studies (BBS) in the Faculty of Management; Bachelor in Science (Computer, Micro Biology, Environmental Science and Technology) in the Faculty of Science and Technology; and Bachelor of Arts (BA) in the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences. It is a co-educational institute affiliated to Tribhuvan University (TU), Nepal.

Committed to its motto 'Education Par Excellence', Campion College aspires to become a centre of learning for young students who are determined to meet the challenges posed by the increasing pace of scientific advancements and modernization. After the completion of SLC, 10+2 of Bachelors at Campion, the students will be eligible to pursue higher education in the related field of their studies, in any university worldwide.

Campion aims at the integral, personal formation of the young. To accomplish this goal special effort are made :

  • To encourage them continually to strive after excellence in every field.
  • To value and judiciously use their freedom.
  • To live, read and serve their fellowmen and country unselfishly.
  • To become agents of needed social change in their country.
In brief, Campion envisions the establishment of an educational institute par excellence; Academically Solid, Socially Relevant and Value Oriented.
