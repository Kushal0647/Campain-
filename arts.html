Faculty Of Humanities & Social Science (Bachelor in Arts)

The faculty of Humanities and Social Science is one of the three faculties at Campion College functions under the Management Board. The Board is guided and supported by Advisory Council,a consortium of National Level Personalities from heterogeneous fields and sectors It offers a 3â€“year Bachelor in Arts (B.A) degree in affilation with Tribhuvan University which is the Nepal's number one University.

The objectives of the course, as put down by Tribhuvan University, are :

  • To aquatint the student with knowledge of recent development in different fields of general and technical studies and equip them with skill.
  • To make the students able to cope with new situations and function as well informed educate persons in both Nepal and foreign countries including those for the SAARC.
  • To make the standard of education higher and more qualitative.
  • To generate knowledge and skills required for the job market.
  • To develop broad-based background for specialization in definite disciplines.

Note :

Candidates applying for admission to three-year B.A. program must have successfully completed Intermediate of 10+2 from any other Board/Universities recognized by the T.U.

Course of Study



Bachelor's in Arts - First Year
Subject Marks
Compulsory English 100
Compulsory Nepali 100
Major Subject I 100
Major Subject II 100
Total 400

Bachelor's in Arts - Second Year
Subject Marks
Compulsory English 100
Major Subject I 200
Major Subject II 200
Total 500

Bachelor's in Arts - Third Year
Subject Marks
Compulsory English 100
Major Subject I 200
Major Subject II 200
Total 500

Total Marks : 400 (First Year) + 500 (Second Year) + 500 (Third Year) = 1400

The major subjects offered at B.A. level are:
Group 1 English
Group 2 Culture
Group 3 Sociology
Group 4 Economics

Note :

Students have to choose two major subjects They are allowed to choose one subject each from any of the groups. Both of their Major Subjects CAN NOT be from the same group.
