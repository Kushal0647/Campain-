Faculty Of Science & Technology (Bachelor in Computer Science)

The faculty of Science and Technology is one of the three faculties at Campion College functions under the Management Board. The Board is guided and supported by Advisory Council,a consortium of National Level Personalities from heterogeneous fields and sectors It offers a 3â€“year Bachelor in Computer Science (B.Sc) degree in affilation with Tribhuvan University which is the Nepal's number one University.

During the first two years students will take computer Science, Mathematics and Physics as full papers. In the third year Computer Science will be the major subject. The syllabus in the Computer Science major is designed to impart in-depth knowledge of the development of principles and theories involved in the development of computer systems mechanism and methods. These will be supplemented by practical works to reinforce these theoretical understanding practical experience projects are included in the main modules of the course with the intention that the student conceptualizes, develops and executes a substantial and worthwhile piece of work to a successful completion.

Note :

Candidates applying for admission to the 3 year B.Sc. in Computer Science program must have successfully completed Intermediate or 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as full papers securing minimum of 50% marks from Higher Secondary Education Board, T.U. or any other Board/Universities recognized by the T.U.
